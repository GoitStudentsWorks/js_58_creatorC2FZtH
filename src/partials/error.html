Favorite cocktails

  • Negroni

    An iconic Italian cocktail, the Negroni features a balanced blend of gin, Campari, and sweet vermouth, known for its bittersweet flavor and ruby-red color. Served over ice with an orange slice, it offers a robust and refreshing taste experience.

Favorite ingredients

  • Campari

    Alcoholic

    Campari is an Italian alcoholic liqueur, considered an apéritif, obtained from the infusion of herbs and fruit in alcohol and water.
